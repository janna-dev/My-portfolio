About Me

Hello! I'm Janyl Legados Santillas, a passionate artist and web design enthusiast. My journey in visual arts began during my early school years, where I developed a deep appreciation for composition, color, and storytelling through drawing and painting. Over time, this passion evolved into an interest in how visual aesthetics can shape user experiences on the web. I draw inspiration from nature, modern architecture, and minimalist design philosophies. Artists like Georgia O'Keeffe and designers such as Dieter Rams have influenced my approach to both traditional and digital media, blending emotion with structure. This portfolio represents my exploration of how traditional art principles—such as balance, contrast, rhythm, and unity—translate into digital spaces through web design.
